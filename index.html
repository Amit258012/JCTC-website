About us

Jayateerth's Commerce Tuition Centre started its journey in 2018 as a small institute to provide education in the field of Commerce for PU I and II years students in the district of Vijayapur. With the blessings of His Holiness Shri Satyatmateertharu and under the guidance of Poojya Madhwacharya Mokashi and the valuable support of several mentors this feat has been achieved.

Accountancy is the subject where everybody counts. Our simple funda is to make every student gain ability to score 100 out of 100 in core subjects of Commerce. Subjects covered here are Accountancy and Economics.

JCTC is more of a homely environment than student-teacher relation. Chapterwise printed materials will be provided and pattern is set in accordance with that of Department of PUE. Guest Lectures will be frequently conducted from experienced resource persons who will be prominently among Textbook Committee members and Exam Evaluators. Motivational speeches and career guidance will be given by well known speakers which will help students in decision making. Exam Tips from Top scorers who topped to the State and District levels in II PU will be an important attraction. Without pressurizing students, occasionally quiz and gamings will be organised.

Once in 3 months, pre-finals will be conducted which depicts board exam pattern. Fees will be reasonable considering the middle class families. Institution is at the heart of the city and has convenient conveyance. Recently, JCTC stepped up towards Coaching of Professional Courses ( Foundation ).As it is well said, let our results speak. In our journey of 5 years, we have attained wonderful 100% result Consistently.
